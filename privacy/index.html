治愈房间 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Overview

治愈房间 is a simple offline relaxation app. It lets you type a short note or choose a picture locally on your device, then use a visual action to let it go.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, track, sell, or share personal data.

Notes and Pictures

Text you type in the app stays on your device during the current app session. If you choose a picture, it is only used locally to display it in the room. The app does not upload your text or pictures to a server.

Accounts and Analytics

The app does not require an account and does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Contact

For support, feedback, or privacy questions, please visit the support page: https://echoscarrie-lab.github.io/calm-room-web/support/
